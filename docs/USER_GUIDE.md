## Introduction

Welcome to **SmartMartX**, your comprehensive online delivery platform connecting customers with local retailers and wholesalers. This guide will help you navigate the platform and make the most of its features.

### User Types

- **üë§ Customers**: Browse and purchase products from local retailers
- **üè™ Retailers**: Manage inventory, sell to customers, and order from wholesalers
- **üè≠ Wholesalers**: Supply bulk products to retailers

## Getting Started

### Registration

#### Step 1: Access the Registration Page
1. Open SmartMartX in your browser
2. Click **"Sign Up"** or **"Register"** button on the homepage

#### Step 2: Choose Registration Method

**Option A: Email Registration**
1. Enter your **email address**
2. Create a **strong password** (minimum 8 characters)
3. Enter your **full name**
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. Check your email for verification link
6. Click the verification link to activate your account

**Option B: Google Sign-In**
1. Click **"Continue with Google"** button
2. Select your Google account
3. Grant necessary permissions
4. You'll be automatically logged in

#### Step 3: Select Your Role
1. After registration, you'll be redirected to the **Role Selection** page
2. Choose one of:
   - **Customer**: For personal shopping
   - **Retailer**: For business owners selling to customers
   - **Wholesaler**: For bulk suppliers to retailers
3. Click **"Continue"**

#### Step 4: Complete Business Information (Retailers & Wholesalers Only)

**For Retailers:**
1. Enter **Business Name**
2. Enter **Business Address** (use Google autocomplete)
3. Set **Delivery Radius** (in kilometers)
4. Click **"Complete Setup"**

**For Wholesalers:**
1. Enter **Business Name**
2. Enter **Business Address** (use Google autocomplete)
3. Set **Minimum Order Value** (optional)
4. Add **Service Areas** (cities you serve)
5. Click **"Complete Setup"**

#### Step 5: Start Using SmartMartX
- You'll be redirected to your personalized dashboard
- Start exploring features based on your role

   - **Rejected**: Not approved
   - **Delivered**: Products received

#### Auto-Inventory Update
When wholesaler confirms your B2B order:
1. Products automatically added to your inventory
2. If product exists, stock quantity increases
3. If new product, it's added with 20% markup
4. Products become available for customers
5. You receive confirmation notification

## Troubleshooting

### Login Issues

**"Invalid email or password"**
- Double-check email spelling
- Ensure caps lock is off
- Try password reset
- Check if using correct login method (email vs Google)

**"Email not verified"**
- Check your email inbox
- Look in spam/junk folder
- Click verification link
- Request new verification email

**Can't receive verification email**
- Check spam folder
- Verify email address is correct
- Wait a few minutes and try again
- Contact support if persistent

### Order Issues

**"Out of stock" when adding to cart**
- Product sold out since you started browsing
- Refresh page for latest stock
- Try different retailer
- Check back later

**Cart items disappeared**
- Cart cleared after 24 hours of inactivity
- Logged out clears cart
- Add items again

**Order not confirmed by retailer**
- Wait for retailer to review
- They receive notification
- May take up to 24 hours
- Contact retailer if urgent

**Can't track delivery**
- Wait for order confirmation first
- Tracking available after confirmation
- Refresh page if not showing
- Check order status

### Payment Issues

**Payment failed**
- Check card details are correct
- Ensure sufficient balance
- Bank may have declined
- Try different payment method
- Use COD as backup

**Payment deducted but order not placed**
- Don't retry payment immediately
- Check "Orders" page
- Payment may be pending
- Contact support with transaction ID
- Refund processed within 5-7 days

### B2B Order Issues (Retailers)

**MOQ not met error**
- Check minimum order quantity requirement
- Increase quantity to meet MOQ
- Example: If MOQ is 10, order at least 10 units

**Order rejected by wholesaler**
- Read rejection reason
- Check if stock unavailable
- Verify MOQ requirements met
- Try different wholesaler
- Contact wholesaler for clarification

**Products not added to inventory**
- Wait for wholesaler to confirm order
- Auto-added only after confirmation
- Check "Pending Products" status
- Refresh inventory page

### Technical Issues

**Page not loading**
- Check internet connection
- Refresh page (F5 or pull down)
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser
- Check if site is under maintenance

**Images not showing**
- Slow internet connection
- Wait for images to load
- Refresh page
- Check if image URL is valid

**Map not loading (in order tracking)**
- Enable location permissions
- Check Google Maps API status
- Refresh page
- Try again later

**Notifications not appearing**
- Check notification permissions
- Refresh page
- Clear browser cache
- Ensure logged in

### Account Issues

**Forgot password**
1. Click "Forgot Password" on login
2. Enter email
3. Check email for reset link
4. Create new password
5. Login with new password

**Want to change role**
- Roles cannot be changed after registration
- Each user can have only one role
- Create a new account for different role
- Contact support for special cases

**Account locked**
- Multiple failed login attempts
- Wait 30 minutes and try again
- Use password reset
- Contact support if persistent

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: Is SmartMartX free to use?**
A: Yes! Registration and using the platform is completely free for all users.

**Q: Do I need to verify my email?**
A: Yes, email verification is required for security and to receive order notifications.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: One account per email address. You can't change roles, so choose carefully during registration.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact support through the platform. Note: Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

### For Customers

**Q: How do I know if a retailer delivers to my location?**
A: Check the retailer's delivery radius. If you're within range, they can deliver. Enable location for automatic checking.

**Q: Can I order from multiple retailers at once?**
A: Yes, but each retailer requires a separate order and checkout process.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Card, UPI, Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery (COD).

**Q: How long does delivery take?**
A: Depends on retailer and distance. Estimated time shown during checkout. Typical: 30 mins - 2 hours.

**Q: Can I cancel my order?**
A: Orders can be cancelled before confirmation. Contact retailer for confirmed orders.

**Q: What if product quality is poor?**
A: Leave feedback and contact retailer. Take photos for documentation.

**Q: Are prices negotiable?**
A: No, prices are fixed as listed by retailers.

### For Retailers

**Q: How do I get products to sell?**
A: Order from wholesalers through the platform. Products automatically added to your inventory upon approval.

**Q: What's the commission or fee?**
A: Currently no platform fees. Future pricing to be announced.

**Q: How do I set product prices?**
A: You set your retail prices. Wholesaler products get automatic 20% markup, which you can adjust.

**Q: Can I sell my own products?**
A: Yes, add products directly to your inventory with your own pricing.

**Q: What if I run out of stock?**
A: Mark products as unavailable. Order more from wholesalers. Stock updates automatically.

**Q: How do payments work?**
A: Customers pay through the platform. Payment processing details in business settings.

**Q: Do I need a business license?**
A: Check local regulations. Platform doesn't verify licenses currently.

### For Wholesalers

**Q: How do I approve B2B orders?**
A: Go to "Orders", review pending orders, and click "Confirm" or "Reject".

**Q: What happens when I approve an order?**
A: Your stock decreases, retailer's inventory increases automatically, and they're notified.

**Q: Why set MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity)?**
A: Ensures economical order sizes, reduces shipping costs, and manages inventory efficiently.

**Q: Can I reject orders?**
A: Yes, provide a reason. Retailer is notified and can try again later.

**Q: How do I manage delivery?**
A: Set service areas in your profile. Update order status as you process and deliver.

**Q: What's the pricing strategy?**
A: Set wholesale prices. Retailers typically add 20-30% markup for retail.

### Technical

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest 2 versions). Mobile browsers supported.

**Q: Do I need to install an app?**
A: No, it's a web application. Works directly in your browser.

**Q: Can I use it offline?**
A: No, internet connection required for all features.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, we use encryption, secure authentication, and database security policies.

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: Securely in cloud databases with automatic backups.

### Support

**Q: How do I contact support?**
A: Through the platform's support feature or contact team members listed in documentation.

**Q: What are support hours?**
A: Currently limited support during project phase. Full support coming soon.

**Q: How do I report a bug?**
A: Contact support with details: what happened, when, and steps to reproduce.

**Q: Can I suggest features?**
A: Yes! We welcome feedback. Use the feedback feature or contact support.
